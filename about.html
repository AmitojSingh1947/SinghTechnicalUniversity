About Us

Singh Technical University

Welcome to our esteemed research university, where our commitment to excellence is matched only by our compassion. Together, we envision a world where knowledge, empathy, and respect converge to create a brighter future for all. Singh Technical University has been enhancing the technology of the country for years. One of Delhi's major engineering school, the Amitoj Singh School of Engineering and Information Technology, provides a variety of fully approved technology programmes, guaranteed placements, and a Centre for Impactful Education. Our Main Campuses are located in London, New York and Washington.
