Undergraduate Programs

Singh Technical University has been enhancing the technology of the country for years. One of Delhi's major engineering school, the Amitoj Singh School of Engineering and Information Technology, provides a variety of fully approved technology programmes, guaranteed placements, and a Centre for Impactful Education

Graduate Programs

Singh Technical University is happy to offer a variety of coed graduate programmes for women and men who are eager to develop their professions and grow as leaders, building on our strong foundations in leadership, information technology, and education. You may achieve your dream of becoming a Management Professional by enrolling in the STU Business Administration programme.

Post Graduation

As one of the biggest engineering schools in Delhi, The STU provides a variety of fully recognised engineering programmes, guaranteed placements, and a Centre for Impactful Education that is technologically advanced. You may achieve your dream of becoming a Software Development Engineer by enrolling in the STU Masters Programme in Computer Science.
